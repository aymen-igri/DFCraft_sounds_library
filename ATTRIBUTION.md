## How to Attribute CC BY Sounds

When displaying or using CC BY licensed sounds, include attribution in the following format:

> "Forest Ambience" by klankbeeld (Freesound.org) -- CC BY 4.0

For projects with a credits screen, list all attributions together:

**Sounds used under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0:**
